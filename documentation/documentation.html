Documentation of the game

Here you can see informations about how to play the game.

Move in the galaxy

Use the click mouse. Dont forget you've got a mini map at the top right of the galaxie screen to help you knowing where you are/go.

Move on a planet

Use the keyboard arrow (Left, Right, Down, Up) to move the player.

Land on a planet

Head to the planet and click on the button "Land" to land your ship on a planet and switch to the planet scene.

Open and use your inventory

Press I on your keyboard. You can click on an item to see its description and the number you got. You can also click on equip to equip a weapon.

Open the quest board

Press L on your keyboard. It shows you if you got a quest, the actual step, the completed step(s) and also the item and money reward.

How to fight

On the fight screen, you've got a button Attack, Repair and Dodge. Every time you click on a button, it uses of your actions you got every turn. It's also written on your screen the number of actions left, your life, your shield, the ennemy's name, ennemy's life and ennemy's shield. At the bottom right of your screen, there's a fight log that shows you every actions of the fight.
